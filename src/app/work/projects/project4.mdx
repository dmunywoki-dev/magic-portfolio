## Overview

The E-Learning Platform supports continuous training for procurement officers and suppliers across Kenya. It provides a flexible, online environment for capacity building, improving compliance and operational efficiency.

## Key Features

- **Course Management:** Admins can create, publish, and update training modules.  
- **Progress Tracking:** Monitors learner performance and certifications.  
- **Interactive Lessons:** Supports quizzes, video lectures, and downloadable resources.  
- **Reporting Tools:** Provides analytics for training outcomes and participation rates.

## Technologies Used

- **Backend:** Django + SQLite  
- **Frontend:** React, Tailwind CSS  
- **Hosting:** AWS EC2, CloudFront CDN  

## Outcome

The platform empowered thousands of users to access training remotely, reducing costs and improving national e-procurement literacy.
