## Overview

This upcoming project focuses on financial inclusion by providing a platform for small business owners to access micro-loans. It uses alternative data points and AI to assess creditworthiness.

## Key Features

- **Instant Credit Scoring:** AI models analyzing transaction history.
- **Peer-to-Peer Lending:** Connects borrowers directly with lenders.
- **Secure Wallet:** Integrated digital wallet for disbursements and repayments.

## Technologies Used

- **Mobile:** Flutter, Dart
- **Backend:** Node.js, Firebase
- **AI:** Python, Scikit-learn

## Outcome

Targeting to facilitate over 10,000 micro-loans in the first year, boosting local economies.
