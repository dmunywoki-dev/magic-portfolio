## Overview

This project aims to revolutionize STEM education by bringing textbooks to life. Students can scan diagrams to see 3D animated models of molecules, physics experiments, and biological systems.

## Key Features

- **3D Visualization:** Interactive models of complex subjects.
- **Gamified Learning:** Quizzes and challenges integrated into the AR experience.
- **Offline Mode:** Content available without constant internet connection.

## Technologies Used

- **AR:** Unity, AR Foundation
- **Mobile:** iOS (Swift), Android (Kotlin)
- **Backend:** GraphQL, Apollo Server

## Outcome

Goal is to increase student engagement and retention rates in science subjects by providing immersive learning tools.
