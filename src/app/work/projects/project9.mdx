## Overview

A system designed to optimize renewable energy usage in off-grid communities. It monitors solar panel output and battery storage levels to prevent outages.

## Key Features

- **Real-time Monitoring:** Live data on energy production and consumption.
- **Predictive Maintenance:** Alerts for hardware degradation or faults.
- **Usage Analytics:** Reports on energy trends to optimize distribution.

## Technologies Used

- **Hardware:** ESP32, Raspberry Pi
- **Cloud:** AWS IoT Core, Lambda
- **Frontend:** Vue.js, D3.js

## Outcome

Expected to improve energy reliability by 30% for remote communities relying on solar power.
