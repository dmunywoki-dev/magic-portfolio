## Overview

**HealthBodyBoost** is a sleek and scalable ecommerce platform designed for health and wellness enthusiasts. It enables customers to easily browse, compare, and purchase premium supplements — from protein powders and vitamins to herbal performance boosters — all in one trusted online store.

## Key Features

- **Product Catalog & Filtering:** Browse supplements by category, goal, or ingredient for a personalized shopping experience.  
- **Secure Checkout:** Integrated payment gateways ensure smooth and safe transactions.  
- **User Accounts & Orders:** Customers can manage profiles, track orders, and view purchase history.  
- **Admin Dashboard:** Simplifies inventory, pricing, and order management.  
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.  

## Technologies Used

- **Backend:** Django REST Framework, PostgreSQL  
- **Frontend:** React.js, Tailwind CSS  
- **Payments & Auth:** Stripe API, JWT Authentication  
- **Cloud & Hosting:** AWS + Nginx  
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics & Firebase  

## Challenges and Learnings

Building a secure and scalable ecommerce system required deep focus on performance optimization and user experience. Implementing real-time inventory tracking and payment validation strengthened transaction reliability and reduced errors.

## Outcome

HealthBodyBoost has provided a fast, modern, and intuitive shopping experience for fitness-focused customers. It continues to expand with features like AI-driven product recommendations and subscription-based supplement plans.
