## Overview

**Bridge** is a digital innovation collaboration platform developed at **@iLabAfrica**, designed to connect innovators, researchers, and industry stakeholders across Kenya. The platform serves as a national hub for sharing ideas, research outputs, and innovation opportunities, enabling collaboration that accelerates commercialization and societal impact.

## Key Features

- **Innovation Repository:** A searchable database of projects, startups, and research innovations across various sectors.  
- **Collaboration Tools:** Facilitates partnerships between academia, industry, and government through profile matching and project co-creation spaces.  
- **Funding & Opportunities:** Curated listings of grants, competitions, and accelerator programs for innovators and researchers.  
- **User Dashboard:** Personalized analytics for users to track engagement, partnerships, and project growth.  
- **Admin Management Panel:** Enables data curation, content moderation, and system analytics for administrators.

## Technologies Used

- **Backend:** Django REST Framework, PostgreSQL  
- **Frontend:** React.js, Tailwind CSS  
- **Authentication:** JWT-based user management and role-based access control  
- **Cloud & Hosting:** AWS + Nginx  
- **Integrations:** Kenya National Innovation Agency (KENIA) APIs and data connectors

## Challenges and Learnings

Coordinating multiple stakeholder needs — from government agencies to startups — required careful UX design and scalable data architecture. Our team implemented modular APIs and a dynamic content model to support diverse data types and workflows.

## Outcome

Bridge successfully streamlined how innovators and organizations in Kenya discover, collaborate, and commercialize solutions. The platform continues to expand, driving national innovation networks and research visibility through open data and strategic partnerships.
